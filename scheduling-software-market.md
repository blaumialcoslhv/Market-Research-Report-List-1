<p><p>Scheduling software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based scheduling software operates online, allowing users to access their scheduling tools from anywhere with an internet connection. This model offers flexibility, automatic updates, and scalability. In contrast, on-premises scheduling software is installed locally on company servers, providing more control over data security and customization. However, it may require more IT resources for maintenance and updates, making it less flexible than its cloud counterpart.</p></p>
