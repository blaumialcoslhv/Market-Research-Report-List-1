<p><ul><li>Tower Server</li><li>Rack Server</li><li>Blade Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data center server market encompasses three primary types: tower servers, rack servers, and blade servers. Tower servers are standalone units resembling traditional desktop PCs, ideal for small businesses with limited space. Rack servers, designed to be mounted in racks, offer efficient use of space and are suitable for larger enterprises due to their scalability. Blade servers are compact modules that slide into a chassis, maximizing density and minimizing power consumption, making them ideal for high-performance computing environments.</p></p>
