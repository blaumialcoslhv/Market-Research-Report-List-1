Carbonate additives improve electrolyte stability and conductivity, essential for high-performance batteries. Ionic liquid additives offer low volatility and thermal stability, enhancing safety and efficiency. Other additives may include polymer-based or metal additives, serving various functions to optimize battery efficiency, longevity, and overall performance.</p></p>
